## Full Description

Bible Chat — Bible verses on your lock screen, daily plans, audio Bible, and a Christian calendar, all in one app 📖

Join millions of Christians using the world's #1 faith app. Made for your busy life — at home, at work, on the go.


📖 MOST-LOVED FEATURES

• Lock Screen Widget — A new Bible verse on your lock screen, every time you check your phone. No need to open anything.

• Daily Plan — A simple spiritual rhythm for your day: a verse, a reflection and a prayer, delivered one step at a time.

• Christian Calendar — Holy days, saints' days and liturgical events, month by month, right on your phone.

• Multiple Bible Translations — NIV, NKJV, NASB, ESV, KJV, NLT, NRSV, RSVCE, Amplified, WEB, CSB and more.

• Audio Bible — Listen to the Bible while you commute, fold laundry, or walk the dog.

• Daily Prayer — Morning and evening prayers, gentle reminders, and a streak to track your habit.

• Christian Meditation — Scripture-based guided meditations for peace, anxiety relief, gratitude, and sleep.

• Sleep Mode — Calming music with ambient sounds (rain, ocean, forest) and a sleep timer. Drift off with God's Word.


👨‍👩‍👧 FOR THE WHOLE FAMILY

• Kids' Bible Stories — Short, captivating stories for little ones with moral lessons.

• Panic Button — Feeling anxious? Tap for guided breathing and a verse of comfort.

• Live Prayer — Share what's on your heart and pray alongside others around the world.

• Send a Blessing — Pick a verse and send it to a friend with a personal message.


🙏 FOR THOSE SEEKING ANSWERS

• Ask the Bible — Ask anything about a passage or theme. Get answers grounded in Scripture with references.

• Study Plans — Healing, anxiety, forgiveness, wisdom — short, focused plans for real life.

• Bible Profiles — Learn from the people of the Bible.

• Bible Trivia — Test what you know through quick, friendly trivia games.


🧘 MEDITATION & WELLNESS

• Guided Christian Meditation — Sessions for peace, anxiety, morning, sleep, gratitude, healing, forgiveness.

• Breathing Exercises — Scripture-synced breathing patterns (4-7-8, box breathing, and more).

• Sleep & Rest Mode — Ambient sounds mixer + calming Scripture music + sleep timer + dim screen mode.

• Mood Tracker — Check in with how you feel and get personalized verse suggestions.


✝️ YOUR WALK WITH GOD, ONE STEP AT A TIME

Whether you go to church every Sunday or you're just finding your way back, Bible Chat meets you where you are — with reverence, not noise. No pressure. No judgment.
